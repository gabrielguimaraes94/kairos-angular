Kairos 360 Academy

Kairos 360 Academy is Gestamp’s internal capability program designed to accelerate digital transformation through standardized learning, practical deployment and measurable performance outcomes.

Vision

Establish a scalable global academy aligned with manufacturing priorities and a consistent skills framework.

360° Approach

Ensure learning is created with standards, implemented in plants, tracked for adoption and improved continuously.

Global Impact

Enable cross-plant alignment, reduce variability and build internal trainers to scale knowledge worldwide.
