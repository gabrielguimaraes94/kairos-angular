Rewards & Recognition

Kairos recognition is based on skill maturity, real application and measurable impact. It is connected to the Kairos Skill Matrix Framework and validated through learning paths and real projects across plants.

K1

Foundation Level

Core foundations completed. Common language and readiness to apply standard practices on the shopfloor.

  • Complete foundation learning paths (Beginner)
  • Demonstrate basic application on the job
  • Validated at Beginner level in the Skill Matrix
K2

Practitioner Level

Practical competence demonstrated through application, improvement initiatives and data-driven execution.

  • Complete role-based learning paths (Intermediate)
  • Deliver a practical case, improvement or standard deployment
  • Validated at Intermediate level in the Skill Matrix
K3

Expert / Champion Level

Advanced impact. Ability to lead implementations, mentor others and deliver measurable business results.

  • Lead a validated AI, Digital or Industry 4.0 project
  • Demonstrate measurable impact (quality, downtime, cost, safety or delivery)
  • Act as local reference for standards and best practices

Become a Kairos Trainer

The Trainer pathway is designed for Experts and Champions who demonstrate the ability to transfer knowledge, support adoption and scale standards across plants.

Expert / Champion → Trainer → Global Referent

This pathway enables global scalability, ensures consistency of standards, and recognizes technical leaders who drive adoption beyond their own plant.

Kairos recognition is not based on course completion alone, but on demonstrated capability, real application and sustained impact across the organization.

A scalable model to retain talent and standardize knowledge globally.
