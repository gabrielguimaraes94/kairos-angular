Industrial Context

Unexpected bearing failures are a major driver of unplanned downtime. Vibration analysis enables early detection by identifying abnormal patterns before failure occurs.

Key Concepts

Amplitude
Frequency
Baseline
Trend
Alarm Threshold
Failure Mode

In practice, you compare current vibration readings against a baseline and track trends to identify degradation.

Real-World Application

  • Detect early bearing defects in motors and gearboxes
  • Support maintenance planning with evidence-based decisions
  • Reduce emergency corrective maintenance and downtime

Shopfloor Task

Select one critical motor and define a baseline vibration reading. Record a second reading after one week and document the trend. If readings exceed threshold, propose an action plan.
