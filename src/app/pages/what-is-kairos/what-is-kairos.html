What is Kairos

Kairos 360 Academy is Gestamp’s capability platform to standardize digital skills, accelerate adoption on the shopfloor and deliver measurable operational impact through a scalable learning model.

Why it exists

Plants evolve at different speeds. Kairos reduces variability by creating a shared learning standard, aligned with manufacturing priorities and real operational needs.

What makes it different

Kairos focuses on implementation, not only content. Each module connects learning to shopfloor execution, measurable outcomes and continuous improvement.

How it scales

A 360° cycle and internal trainer model allow cross-plant replication, certification and governance with visibility for leadership.

Kairos Pillars

Standardization
Common learning standards, role-based paths and skill coverage across plants.
Execution
Hands-on training connected to real manufacturing use cases and deployment.
Measurement
Training records, adoption tracking and performance outcomes visible to leadership.
Continuous Improvement
Feedback loops to improve standards, modules and implementation over time.
